FIELD NOTES

I've been fascinated by cybersecurity since before I knew what to call it. I was the kid picking apart systems just to see how they worked, running Linux in high school while everyone else was on Windows. CV came later, through a professor who taught it like it was the most important field in the world. Turns out, he wasn't wrong. The two clicked for me: if you understand how systems break, you build smarter ones. That overlap is where I work.
